About MiniMind Learning

MiniMind Learning is a home-based tuition service run by a passionate educator with over 5 years of teaching experience.

I provide personalized one-on-one and small group tutoring sessions for students from Nursery to Grade 5. Each child learns differently, and I focus on making learning simple, engaging, and effective for every student.

Whether it’s improving reading, math, or writing skills, We ensures that young learners feel confident and supported every step of the way.
